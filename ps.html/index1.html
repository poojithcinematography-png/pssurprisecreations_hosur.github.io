About Us

We didn’t start with a big office, a big team, or big promises. Just a simple idea—make people feel special in ways they never forget. That’s how PS Surprise Creations began. Today, we are a growing team of passionate creators based in Hosur, united by one goal: turning ordinary moments into meaningful experiences. What started as small surprise setups and birthday decorations has now evolved into a creative space where emotions, design, and execution come together seamlessly. For us, an event isn’t just decoration. It’s a memory in the making. Every balloon we place, every light we set, every detail we design—it all has a purpose. Whether it’s a surprise birthday, a proposal, a baby shower, or a corporate event, we bring the same energy, care, and attention to every setup. Because we believe every celebration should feel personal, never pre-made.


What makes us different?

We don’t just “manage events.” We feel them. We understand that behind every event, there’s a story—someone planning a heartfelt surprise, a brand building its presence, or a family celebrating a special milestone. And we treat every moment with the importance it deserves. “Our strength lies in our team”
